# ExChek ECP / Policy & Training Generator

Generate **tailored Export Compliance Program (ECP) documents**, **SOPs**, and **training outlines** from your company’s footprint, product mix, and risk profile. Aligns with BIS and DDTC guidance and maps screening, classification, and licensing into CRM/ERP/agents.

**Outputs:** ECP document (BIS nine elements), Export Compliance SOP(s), role-based training outline.

**Inputs:** Company footprint, product mix, risk profile; optional existing controls and systems (CRM, ERP, agents).

**Why use it:** Move from “tool” to structuring your whole compliance program; audit-ready ECP and training foundation. Free to use; optional donation.

**Docs:** https://docs.exchek.us
