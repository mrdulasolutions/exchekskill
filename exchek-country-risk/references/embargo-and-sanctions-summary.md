# Embargo and Sanctions Quick Reference

Use for consistent embargo/sanctions wording in the country risk one-pager. Check 15 CFR Part 746 and OFAC for current scope; this table is indicative.

---

## EAR comprehensive or severe restrictions (15 CFR Part 746)

| Destination | Part 746 | License exceptions |
|-------------|----------|--------------------|
| Cuba | § 746.2 | Severely restricted; § 740.2 bars most. |
| Iran | § 746.7 | Severely restricted; § 740.2 bars most. |
| North Korea | § 746.4 | Severely restricted; § 740.2 bars most. |
| Syria | § 746.9 | Severely restricted; § 740.2 bars most. |
| Russia / Belarus | § 746.5, § 746.6 | Many items require license; exceptions limited. |
| Crimea Region of Ukraine | § 746.8 | Comprehensive embargo. |

**Country Group E:1** (Supplement No. 1 to Part 740): Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria (and as updated — Russia/Belarus/Crimea may be included). License exceptions are largely barred under § 740.2(a)(1) for E:1.

---

## OFAC (indicative)

- **Comprehensive sanctions programs** (most transactions prohibited unless authorized): Iran, North Korea, Syria, Cuba, Crimea, Donetsk/Luhansk (and others per OFAC).
- **List-based** (SDN, SSI, etc.): All countries; screening required. Country risk one-pager should state "Comprehensive" vs "List-based only" where applicable.

**Source:** [OFAC Sanctions Programs](https://ofac.treasury.gov/sanctions-programs-and-country-information).
