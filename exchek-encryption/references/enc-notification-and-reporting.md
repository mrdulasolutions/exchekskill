# ENC notification and reporting (BIS/NSA) — prep only

This reference supports the ExChek encryption skill in determining **when BIS/NSA notification** or **annual self-classification report** is required for encryption items, and **what to prepare**. The skill does **not** perform actual filing or submission; it identifies obligations and prepares content for the user to submit.

**Regulatory basis:** 15 CFR 740.17 (License Exception ENC); 15 CFR 742.15 (Encryption items); BIS encryption guidance and annual self-classification pages. Current procedures: [BIS Encryption](https://www.bis.doc.gov/index.php/policy-guidance/encryption) and [BIS Annual Self-Classification](https://www.bis.doc.gov/index.php/policy-guidance/encryption/4-reports-and-reviews/a-annual-self-classification).

---

## 1. BIS/NSA notification and encryption registration

- **When required:** Export or reexport of encryption items under License Exception ENC often requires **encryption registration** or **notification** to BIS (and, where applicable, coordination with NSA). The exact trigger depends on the ENC sub-paragraph (e.g. 740.17(b)(1)) and product type. Post-2016 rules (e.g. 81 FR 35586) reduced many 30-day technical review waiting periods; **immediate authorization** after electronic submission of an encryption registration is available for many products.
- **Submission:** Notifications and registrations are submitted to the **ENC Encryption Request Coordinator** (e.g. BIS, with NSA review where required). Contact and current procedures are on the BIS encryption web pages.
- **Skill output:** State whether notification/registration is **required** or **not required** for the product in question; list what the user should **prepare** (e.g. product description, ECCN, encryption parameters, destination scope). Do **not** perform the actual submission.

---

## 2. Annual self-classification report

- **740.17(b)(1):** Exporters of certain encryption products under License Exception ENC (e.g. 740.17(b)(1)) must submit an **annual self-classification report** to BIS, unless an exemption applies.
- **March 2021 rule and mass market:** The March 29, 2021 rule eliminated self-classification **reporting requirements** for **most mass market products** (e.g. ECCN 5A992.c and 5D992.c as described in the rule). Mass market **components** and **executable software** may still require self-classification reporting; confirm against current 740.17 and BIS guidance.
- **CCATS products:** Products that BIS has classified via a **Commodity Classification Request (CCATS)** do **not** require a self-classification report for that product; the skill can note “BIS classified via CCATS — annual self-classification report not required for this product.”
- **Skill output:** Conclude whether the **annual self-classification report is required** or **not required**; if required, state what to **prepare** (e.g. product list, ECCNs, encryption description, annual submission deadline). Do **not** perform the actual filing.

---

## 3. Prep only — no filing by the skill

- The skill **prepares** the memo and identifies **what** must be submitted and **when** (per current rules). The **user** is responsible for:
  - Actual submission of notifications, registrations, or annual reports to BIS/NSA.
  - Timing (e.g. before first export under ENC, or by annual deadline).
  - Accuracy and completeness of submissions.
- Recommend that the user verify current procedures and deadlines at [BIS Encryption](https://www.bis.doc.gov/index.php/policy-guidance/encryption) and consult counsel or a qualified compliance professional for high-stakes or uncertain cases.
