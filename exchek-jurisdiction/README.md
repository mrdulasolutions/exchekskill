# ExChek ITAR vs. EAR Jurisdiction Skill

Determines whether an item is subject to **ITAR** (State/DDTC) or **EAR** (Commerce/BIS) through a guided questionnaire covering:

- Other-agency jurisdiction (e.g. NRC, DOE, DEA)
- United States Munitions List (USML), 22 CFR Part 121
- "Specially designed" for a defense article (22 CFR § 121(d))
- Items subject to the EAR (15 CFR § 734.3)

Produces a short **Jurisdiction Determination Memo** with recommended jurisdiction and **next steps** (e.g. contact DDTC vs. run BIS/ExChek classification). Does not assign ECCN or USML category — that is done by the ExChek classification skill or DDTC.

**When to use:** "Is this ITAR or EAR?", "Jurisdiction check for this item", "Do we need to go to DDTC or BIS?"
