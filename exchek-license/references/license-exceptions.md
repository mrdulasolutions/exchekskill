# EAR license exceptions — quick reference

15 CFR Part 740. **§ 740.2** restricts use of all exceptions (e.g., embargoed countries, end use/end user, General Prohibitions). Always check § 740.2 first.

| Exception | Citation | Typical use | When you cannot use |
|-----------|----------|-------------|----------------------|
| **LVS** (Low Value Shipments) | § 740.3 | Shipments below value threshold per ECCN | Destination in Country Group E:1 or E:2; restricted end use/end user; item not eligible per ECCN |
| **GBS** (Shipments to Group B countries) | § 740.4 | Eligible items to Group B countries | Country not in Group B; ECCN not eligible; § 740.2 bars |
| **CIV** (Civil end-users) | § 740.5 | Certain items for civil end use in Group D:1 | End use/end user not civil; country not eligible; § 740.2 bars |
| **TSR** (Technology and Software under restriction) | § 740.6 | Eligible tech/software to Group B | Not tech/software; country or eligibility not met; § 740.2 bars |
| **TMP** (Temporary imports, exports, reexports) | § 740.9 | Temporary shipment (e.g., exhibition, repair) | Not temporary; duration/conditions exceeded; § 740.2 bars |
| **RPL** (Servicing and replacement of parts) | § 740.10 | One-for-one replacement or servicing | Not replacement/servicing; conditions not met; § 740.2 bars |
| **ENC** (Encryption) | § 740.17 | Encryption items per 740.17 | Not encryption; or 740.17 conditions/restrictions not met |

**Country Groups:** Supplement No. 1 to Part 740. **Embargoes:** Part 746; § 740.2(a) often bars exceptions for embargoed destinations.

**EAR99:** Many exceptions (e.g., LVS, GBS where applicable) can apply to EAR99; check each exception and § 740.2.

For full text and conditions, use Part 740 structure from the API or eCFR and cite the specific section (e.g., § 740.3(b) for LVS value limits).
